# 🇺🇿 UZBEKISTAN
**FIFA Ranking:** 74th | **Coach:** Srecko Katanec (Slovenian) | **System:** 5-3-2 / 3-5-2 defensive | **Group:** K

---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** Deep block defensive structure — 5 defenders, 3 midfielders, 2 forwards. Sit compact and look for counter-attack moments. Not a possession team (33% vs Colombia). First World Cup appearance as an independent nation.
- **Masharipov OUT (CRITICAL):** Captain and primary creative midfielder Otabek Masharipov is injured and out of the tournament. He was the player who connected defense to attack and provided the creative link in their counter-attack system. Without him, Uzbekistan's attacking creativity is severely limited.
- **Khusanov yellow card risk:** Man City's Abdukodir Khusanov plays at CB/FB for Uzbekistan. Was booked vs Colombia and committed multiple fouls on wide attackers. One more yellow = suspended. Against Portugal's wide players (Neto, Leao), he's the primary defensive matchup and his discipline is a live concern.
- **Limited attacking output vs possession teams:** Had 0.02 xG at halftime vs Colombia — literally no threatening presence in the first 45 minutes against a possession side. Their entire attacking threat manifests in the second half when they're either behind and must push, or when counter opportunities arise.
- **First WC for this generation:** Never before won a World Cup match. The occasion itself is significant.

---

## Key Players

- **Shomurodov** — Genoa (Serie A) — CF / Target man — 92 caps, 44 international goals. Physical, holds up play. His volley was parried (leading to Fayzullaev's goal) vs Colombia. Still a real aerial and physical threat.
- **Fayzullaev** — Forward / Midfielder — Scored vs Colombia (60') from a scramble rebound after Shomurodov's volley was parried. Takes free kicks and corners. Without Masharipov, he's the most likely to create something.
- **Abdukodir Khusanov** — Man City — CB / FB — Best technical player in the squad. On yellow card. Aggressive and can be exposed by pace. His rash challenges are a card-risk vs Portugal's width.
- **Otabek Masharipov** — OUT (injury) — Captain. The most creative player. His absence removes Uzbekistan's primary attacking mechanism in transition.
- **Jalal Hassan** — GK — Has made some important saves to keep Uzbekistan in games but also has been beaten by shots his defensive teammates thought were covered.
