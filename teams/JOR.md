---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** Defensive block, sit deep, counter-attack. Jordan are not an attacking team — their strength is in organized defensive shape and pace on the break.
- **Punching above their weight:** Despite the 1-3 loss to Austria, Jordan equalized at 50' and had 4 shots on target — more than Austria's 3. They caused Austria problems before the 76' OG opened the floodgates.
- **Counter-attack threat:** When Jordan do attack, Hamza Al-Dardour and Mousa Al-Taamari provide pace and directness on the break.

## Key Players
