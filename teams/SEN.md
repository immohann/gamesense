# 🇸🇳 SENEGAL
**FIFA Ranking:** 18th | **Coach:** Aliou Cisse | **System:** 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1 | **Group:** I

---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** Athletic, physical, direct. Sadio Mane as the focal point. Press high, win second balls, attack with pace in wide areas. Not a particularly technical team but very difficult to bully physically.
- **Mane's leadership:** Mane is the captain and the attacking mechanism. When he is on form, Senegal are threatening. When he's off, the attacking output drops sharply.
- **Defensive resilience:** Tough to score against when organized. But France exposed them in the second half of MD1 after the tactical adjustment.

---

## Key Players

- **Sadio Mane** — Al Nassr — CF / Left Wing — Captain. The attacking focal point. Pace, direct running, clinical finisher. His form determines Senegal's ceiling.
- **Ismaila Sarr** — Crystal Palace — Right Wing — Pace, direct, creates one-on-one situations. Secondary wide threat.
- **Pape Matar Sarr** — Spurs — Central Mid — Dynamic, box-to-box. Arrives late.
- **Kalidou Koulibaly** — Al Hilal — CB — The defensive anchor. Physical, dominant, elite at this level despite age.
- **Edouard Mendy** — Chelsea (TBC) — GK — Quality shot-stopper.

## Betting Patterns

- **Over/Under tendency:** Over when facing quality opposition who push forward — Senegal can score from counter-attacks.
- **BTTS tendency:** Yes vs top-quality teams who must push forward. Their counter-attack quality (Mane/Sarr pace) means they score when space opens.
- **⚠️ Key watch:** Mane fitness and sharpness. If he is at 100%, Senegal are a genuine threat on the counter. First half defensive organization is their strength.
