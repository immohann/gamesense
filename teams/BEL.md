# 🇧🇪 BELGIUM
**FIFA Ranking:** 3rd | **Coach:** Domenico Tedesco | **System:** 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1 | **Group:** G

---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** Possession-based, patient build-up, wide stretching through De Bruyne and Doku, finishing through Lukaku. Quality is top-tier but questions around age and cohesion persist.
- **Lukaku impact sub:** Lukaku equalized vs Egypt coming off the bench late — the impact sub role has been established. He is NOT starting every game but is a confirmed game-changer from the bench.
- **Doku pace:** If Doku starts and is fit, Belgium have genuine wide pace to exploit defensive lines. His presence or absence changes Belgium's counter-attack threat significantly.
- **De Bruyne creative engine:** When fit and sharp, De Bruyne's through-ball quality is world-class. The question is always whether he is at 100%.

---

## Key Players

- **Kevin De Bruyne** — Man City — CM / Playmaker — The creative hub. Through-balls, set piece delivery, range of passing. When he is at 100%, Belgium are a different team.
- **Romelu Lukaku** — Napoli — CF — Impact sub confirmed vs Egypt. Physical target man. Still dangerous when he comes on and defenders have been tiring.
- **Leandro Trossard** — Arsenal — Wide / Forward — Creative wide option, clinical in tight spaces.
- **Yannick Carrasco / Jeremy Doku** — Wide pace options. Doku in particular is extremely direct and fast.
- **Arthur Theate / Wout Faes** — CBs — Organized defensive unit.
- **Koen Casteels** — GK — Consistent performer.
