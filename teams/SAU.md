# 🇸🇦 SAUDI ARABIA
**FIFA Ranking:** 56th | **Coach:** Roberto Mancini | **System:** 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1 | **Group:** H

---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** Organized defensive shape, compact mid-block, look to counter through Saleh Al-Shehri and Salem Al-Dawsari's pace. Mancini's system is disciplined but Saudi's quality drops significantly vs top European/South American opposition.
- **Spain humiliation:** 0-4 to Spain in MD1 — 4 goals conceded, 0 scored, 0 shots on target. This was a complete tactical mismatch. Spain's possession and movement dismantled Saudi's shape entirely.
- **Uruguay draw:** Held Uruguay to 1-1 in MD1 (from Uruguay's perspective) — conceded a set piece goal but scored from a Saudi counter that Uruguay's high press created space for. Shows Saudi can punish teams that commit forward.
