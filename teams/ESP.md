# 🇪🇸 SPAIN
**FIFA Ranking:** 2nd | **Coach:** Luis De La Fuente | **System:** 4-3-3 possession | **Group:** H

---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** The definitive possession team. 74%+ possession, short passing, high pressing to win the ball back immediately, overloads in wide areas through fullbacks and inverted wingers. The system that Cape Verde neutralized with a deep block (0-0) but that dismantled Saudi Arabia 4-0.
- **Pedri / Gavi / Yamal as attacking engine:** The triangle of youth and technical quality. Lamine Yamal (17yo) is the most exciting player in the tournament alongside Mbappe and Haaland.
- **Deep-block vulnerability:** Cape Verde's 0-0 showed Spain can be neutralized by a disciplined compact block. They created few clear chances despite 74% possession. The pattern is consistent: great vs teams who play, struggle to unlock the disciplined 5-4-1.

---

## Key Players

- **Lamine Yamal** — Barcelona — Right Wing — 17 years old, the most technically gifted player in the tournament. Direct, creative, turns defenders, scores and assists. The standout talent.
- **Pedri** — Barcelona — CM — The creative hub. Through-balls, positioning, press. Spain's brain.
- **Alvaro Morata** — Atletico — CF — Physical, holds up, aerial. Links play for Yamal and Pedri.
- **Fabian Ruiz / Rodri** — Midfield quality.
- **Marc-Andre ter Stegen** — GK — Reliable, sweeping keeper.

## Tournament Form (2026 WC)

- **MD1:** vs Saudi Arabia (4-0) — Spain dominant. 4 goals, 0 conceded, Saudi 0 shots on target. **Key observation: Against teams who sit deep but don't have the defensive organization of Cape Verde, Spain are devastating.**
- **MD2 note:** Cape Verde 0-0 in MD1 showed Spain's limitation vs elite defensive blocks.
- **MD3:** vs Uruguay — TBD. This is the Group H decider.

## Betting Patterns

- **Over vs possession-passive teams:** Yes when the opponent tries to play with them (like Saudi Arabia).
- **Under vs deep blocks:** Spain vs Cape Verde 0-0 is the template. When the opponent sits at 4-5-1 and defends deep, Spain generate good xG but struggle to convert.
- **⚠️ Key watch:** Spain vs Uruguay (MD3) — Uruguay's pressing style creates a more open game than Spain typically faces. Could be a high-scoring, open affair.
