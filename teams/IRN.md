# 🇮🇷 IRAN
**FIFA Ranking:** 20th | **Coach:** Amir Ghalenoei | **System:** 4-2-3-1 / 4-4-2 | **Group:** G

---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** Compact, organized defensive block with quick transitions. Press high at times but primarily a counter-attacking team. Disciplined shape — hard to break down even for quality opposition.
- **Defensive resilience:** Held Belgium to 0-0 in MD2 — Belgium had 60%+ possession and couldn't score. This is a genuine organized defensive unit, not just a poor team.
- **Physical and direct:** When they do attack, Iran are direct — looking for balls over the top to their forward runners rather than intricate build-up.
- **Transition threat:** Sardar Azmoun is their quality outlet — can hold up play or run in behind. When Iran get transition moments, they are dangerous.
