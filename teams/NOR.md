# 🇳🇴 NORWAY
**FIFA Ranking:** 33rd | **Coach:** Stale Solbakken | **System:** 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1 | **Group:** I

---

## Identity & DNA

- **Playing style:** Direct, physical, built around Haaland as the focal point. Norway don't overcomplicate — wide balls into Haaland's runs, second balls from his knock-downs, and Odegaard's creativity from deep. Simple but devastatingly effective when Haaland is finishing.
- **Haaland as the system:** Everything Norway do offensively routes through Haaland. Wide deliveries, through-balls, knockdowns — his runs define where every other player positions. Without Haaland, Norway are a competent but unremarkable team.
- **Physical and pressing:** Norway press high and are physically imposing. Direct balls win second balls. Not a technical team in possession.

---

## Key Players

- **Erling Haaland** — Man City — CF — The best CF in the world. Brace vs Iraq (29', 42'). Impossible to defend when service is good. Holds up, runs behind, aerial dominance. THE scoring mechanism.
- **Martin Odegaard** — Arsenal — AM / Captain — Creative hub from deep. Passes, drives, creates space. The second most important player in the system.
- **Antonio Nusa / Alexander Sorloth** — Wide/Forward options supporting Haaland.
- **Sander Berge** — Midfield anchor — Physical, screens, wins aerial balls.
