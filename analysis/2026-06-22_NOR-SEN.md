1. **Cluster's Over lean assumes Senegal attack openly — group table says they won't.** Must-not-lose posture = defensive setup first. → Over 2.5 confidence drops, Under lean.
2. **Haaland vs Iraq ≠ Haaland vs Koulibaly.** 11-of-12 shots inside the box was against the worst defense in the tournament. Koulibaly at 35 is still elite aerially and positionally. → Norway win confidence stays Medium, not High.
3. **Idrissa Gueye screening Odegaard is the factor nobody mentions.** If Gueye wins the midfield battle, Haaland's service drops dramatically and Norway's mechanism breaks. → Under lean strengthens.

5. **Draw is structurally supported by BOTH teams' group positions.** Norway comfortable with 4pts going into France. Senegal happy to take a point. When both teams' optimal minimum is the same outcome (draw), the cluster's win lean is structurally wrong. → Draw at +230 is the value call.
